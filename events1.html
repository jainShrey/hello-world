Events(2014-15)


Year :   2015-16   2014-15

Chemipedia

Quizzes have been an archaic method of testing one‘s general under-standing and aptitude. They not only encourage the students to achieve academic excellence and increase their awareness of the world around them, but are also viewed as the pursuit of trivial knowledge. In its unceasing endeavor to assess and proliferate the knowledge of the existing members, the IIChE UPES Student Chapter organized Chemipedia, a quiz contest on February 19, 2015. The event was conducted in three rounds, each team participating in a faction of two. While the preliminary round focused on examining the quantitative aptitude via a pen and paper quiz comprising of 35 ques-tions; the subsequent rounds were devoted to probing into the partici-pants‘ minds to scrutinize their domain knowledge by means of verbal quiz sessions. After a fiery final round, Prakhar & Gopal emerged victori-ous. The event received an exuberant response with over 50 teams par-ticipating to win the battle of sheer intellect.

ChemBlem

The event ChemBlem was organized on February 19, 2015. ChemBlem was an event that tested the students‘ ability to identify and retain some selected brands from the endless ocean of companies. The event witnessed exceptional response with students being hooked to the screens, waiting for the next question to pop across the boars, thus using their grey matter in the process. The event was successful in Its mission to increase student familiarity with assorted technical brands across the globe.

Chemi-call : Paper Presentation

While the need to write a technical paper is unparalleled in a research centric industry like ours, it can also be an extremely daunting and brainstorming task for most. In order to acquaint its members with the know-hows of writing a technical paper and augmenting student inter-est in research and their domain, the IIChE UPES Student Chapter orga-nized Chemi-call, a Paper Presentation Competition on February 21,2015. The event witnessed an overwhelming participation not only from the second and third year students, but also from the freshers who participated with exceptional vigor and zest. Chemi-call provided a platform to eager and talented scholars to showcase their remarkable skills and flair. Technical papers on topics ranging from Alternate Source of Energy, Advance Technologies and Environmental and Safety Meth-ods to Nanotechnology were presented with equal enthusiasm. On the grounds of quality of content ,innovation, presentation skills and accu-racy of the content, the paper authored by Mr. Ankit Tiwari was ad-judged to be the best amongst the rest.

End of the year ceremony

The IIChE UPES Student Chapter organized an End of the year ceremony for its extant Committee members. The event scripted the completion of another beguiling year for the Chapter and celebrates the colossal amount of hard work put in by the members in the time span of another academic session. An exclusive cere-mony was organized for the unabridged Committee on April 24, 2015. Additionally, the event was ornament-ed by the distinguished guests from the Chemical Department, including Dr. S.K Gupta, Dr. P. Vijay, Dr. Das along with Mr. Kumar Gaurav. The celebration served to become a platform for the freshly designated members to interact with both the faculty members as well as with the existing members of the Committee. The session witnessed an all-embracing interaction between the members and also with the faculty members, wherein the faculty mem-bers shared their inciting experiences, mentoring the students in the process to work with utmost diligence, proficiency and brio, to conquer zeniths for the Chapter. Furthermore, as a part and parcel of the End of the Year Ceremony, the Committee got a fortuitous prospect to the bid adieu to the Core-Committee members of the Class of 2011-15, Mr. Kanishk Mehta, Ms. Sonal Bhadauriya and Mr. Arvind Singh. A trivial ritual of the transfer of official duties took place wherein a new set of office bearers took charge of the functioning of the Chapter, marking the end of an era, and foundation of a new one. The event concluded with the cutting of cake and a small dinner subsequently. The day not only illustrated the dusk of a year of effort, but provided a sneak peek into the captivating dawn of the forthcoming year.
