Guest Lecture 1 - Dr. Arvind P. Kudchadker

Guest of honor: Dr. Arvind P. Kudchadker, Professor Emeritus, IIT, Bombay

The first guest lecture organized by IIChE UPES Student Chapter in October 2014 was graced by the august presence of Dr. Arvind P. Kudchadker, Professor Emeritus, IIT, Bombay; Gujarat State Fertilizer Corporation Visiting Chair Professor in Chemical Engineering, DDIT, Nadiad and Wholetime Director, Panandiker R&D Pvt Ltd., Goa. Dr. Kudchadker delivered a highly enlighten-ing and informative lecture on "Exciting times - Challenges & Opportunities" & "Creating a New Technology Institute" in Octo-ber 2014. By employing a number of illustrious examples Dr. Kud-chadker discussed a wide range of topics, from the available opportunities in the industry, propagation of scientific attitude to the need of establishing a better a institute to inculcate a scien-tific attitude among the masses.
